About me

I'm a family-first man who loves to build software products. I founded my first company, "Hivemind Labs", in 2010 with a great friend and our shared belief that belief that:

"Products should solve problems, design should have a purpose, and the greatest ideas are born from necessity and collaboration."

For the past decade I've used this as a foundational truth for a career in software and product development. I've partnered with small businesses, mega-churches, grant-funded non-profits and Fortune 500 companies and more to help lead and direct product development teams and initiatives.

In Aug of 2019, I co-founded a product company named Box Products, a web application firm specializing in software for the InsureTech and FinTech markets. In my spare time, I keep my product kills sharp by consulting via my freelance company, Product Matter.
